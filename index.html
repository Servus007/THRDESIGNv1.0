About Us

THR Design is founded by Robert Theunissen. It's started as a workplace for simple repair fot agriculture machines or applications. By time more and more customers came by with technical challenges. Therefore investments were done in better and professional equipment to answeer the high demand quality. By now the workshop where it once started is an dully equiped workplace for build and repair work.

Who We Are

Our team exist of people who have a passion and motivation for solving electro-mechanical problems. We not only love to repair machines, we are really in to designing, building and operate them.

What We Do

We build and repair agricultural machines, for special needs, we also build state of the art CNC-machines at an affordable price. We love to do automatisation and designing projects from scratch.

Why Choose Us

We are a very enthousiastic and passionate team, where each member has his own important input and technical skills, which makes us very compatible.
